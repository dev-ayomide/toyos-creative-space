Giggles Platform

Helping busy parents find, book and trust the right daycare.

Overview

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

Nov 2023

Tools

Figma, FigJam, Miro — High Fidelity, Prototype

Deliverables

Research, Visual Design, Web Design, Mobile-Responsiveness

Summary

This is a home-based daycare that specialises in loving and caring for infants and toddlers aged 6 weeks to 24 months. Parents spend hours researching childcare options, worrying about safety and reliability. Giggles is designed to give parents a trusting platform where they can find, book, and confidently leave their little ones in great hands.

The product was designed around three core concerns every parent carries: safety, transparency, and peace of mind. Every design decision was filtered through the question — does this make a parent feel more confident?
