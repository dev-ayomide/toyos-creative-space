About

I'm a Product designer with a slightly unconventional journey into design. I started with a Mass Communications Degree from the University of Ilorin, but somewhere between studying Media theories and creating content, I discovered my true calling: making digital products that people actually enjoy.

I do not think design is just about making pretty pixels (though I obsess over a perfect corner radius like it's my part-time job). I see design as the bridge between complex problems and simple solutions, making digital experiences intuitive and genuinely delightful.

Using both the systematic thinking of a Product Design Engineer and the creative soul of an Illustrator. Every project gets the full treatment: Research, Prototypes, Wireframes, Usability testing and Perfect execution.

Feel free to reach out to me for a chat. Thank you for stopping by 💜
