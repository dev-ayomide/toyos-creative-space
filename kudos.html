Summary

KUDOS is a smart budgeting app built specifically for freelancers and gig workers. Unlike traditional banking apps that were designed for people with steady monthly salaries. Kudos was built for people whose income comes in unpredictably (sometimes a lot, sometimes very little).

Kudos is the future of finance, it is an emotional experience that deals with decentralized access,breaking barriers and giving invisible economies a digital footprint.

Reflections

This project challenged me to communicate emotions through illustrations not through words alone. Keeping Jamal's environments consistent across the scenes was one of the most deliberate decisions I made. The same living room in Scene 1 and Scene 4 creates a powerful visual contrast — same space, completely different emotional atmosphere. Color played a huge role in this too.

What I am most proud of is the emotional distance between Scene 1 and Scene 6. When you see where Jamal started and where he ended up. That feeling was the entire goal of this illustration system.

KUDOS is not just a budgeting app. It is proof that the right support can give someone their life back. This project was my attempt to show what that looks like. 💜
